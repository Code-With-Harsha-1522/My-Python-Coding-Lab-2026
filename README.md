## 🚀 Amazing Features Loaded:
* **⏱️ Tik-Tiki Ji (Live Timer):** A real 30-second countdown with a ticking sound effect on every second!
* **👥 Audience Poll Lifeline:** Stuck on a hard question? Call the audience and get live percentage data!
* **📞 50-50 Lifeline:** Instantly makes two wrong answers disappear under pressure.
* **🎉 Confetti Explosion:** Double-tap on correct answers and unlock grand celebration emojis!
* **🕉️ Divine Background:** Beautifully kitted with a glowing Mahadev graphic for a premium feel.
* **🌟 Neon Hover Glow:** Fully kitted with glowing buttons that light up when you move your mouse.

---

## 🛠️ Technologies Used:
* **HTML5:** Crafting the clean and premium structure.
* **CSS3:** Designing neon glowing effects and dark gaming aesthetics.
* **JavaScript (ES6):** Powering the countdown logic, lifelines, confetti, and browser-synthesized audio beep engine.
